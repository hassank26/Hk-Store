HK. Store started as a final year uni project — a simple idea that good clothing shouldn't cost a fortune or come at the planet's expense. We hand-picked every fabric, tested every fit, and launched with just 12 pieces.

Two years later we're still the same small team, still obsessed with the details, still shipping from the same warehouse. Just slightly bigger now.

Every piece is designed with three things in mind: does it look good, does it feel good, and will it still hold up in three years. If it doesn't pass all three — it doesn't make the cut.
